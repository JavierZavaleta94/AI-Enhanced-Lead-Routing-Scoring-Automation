Hubspot AI Qualiying Form

This project captures leads submitted through a HubSpot form (demo link included) and enriches them with AI-driven scoring, qualification, recommended next actions, and insight summaries. The workflow updates HubSpot contacts, logs all data in Google Sheets for reporting, sends Outlook notifications, and triggers Slack alerts for high-priority leads. Hidden URL parameters like Medium, Campaign, Source, Content, Term, Previous Website, and Current Website are also captured to track campaign attribution.
